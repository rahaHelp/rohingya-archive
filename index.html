🔥 Genocide Aftermath

In August 2017, Myanmar military burned 354 Rohingya villages. Families watched their homes destroyed. 700,000+ fled with nothing. This is what they left behind.

GENOCIDE EVIDENCE

🏥 Health Crisis

NGO hospitals only treat normal illness. For serious conditions - diabetes, surgery, blood diseases - refugees need private hospitals. But they have no money. Many die from treatable diseases.

HEALTH EMERGENCY
📚

Photo 3: No School

📚 Education Crisis

Rohingya children cannot attend Bangladesh schools. NGO classes only teach basic literacy. No higher education, no bachelor degrees, no computer skills, no nursing training. A generation is losing its future.

EDUCATION EMERGENCY
🍚

🍚 Food Crisis

WFP cut food rations from $12 to $6 per person per month. New arrivals since 2024 are not registered with UNHCR and receive NOTHING. Families beg for food. Children go to sleep hungry.

FOOD EMERGENCY
🏚️

🏚️ Shelter Crisis

Refugee shelters are made of bamboo and plastic. Every monsoon, roofs leak and walls collapse. Families huddle under wet blankets. No money to buy new tarpaulin or bamboo poles.

SHELTER EMERGENCY
⚰️

⚰️ Boat Crisis

Desperate refugees pay smugglers $2,000-$4,000 for dangerous sea journeys. 457 died or went missing in just 5 months (Jan-May 2025). Bodies wash up on shores. Families never know if loved ones survived.

BOAT EMERGENCY
